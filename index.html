Your dashboard

Effortlessly see a real-time display of essential vehicle parameters and performance metrics. This comprehensive overview helps users monitor their vehicle's performance, diagnose potential issues, and stay informed about crucial aspects of their car's health and efficiency.

DTC Codes

Users can easily view any trouble codes, which are specific codes that indicate potential issues or malfunctions in various systems of the vehicle. The app provides a clear and concise description of each trouble code, helping users understand the nature of the problem. Additionally, the app offers a convenient option to clear or reset these trouble codes, providing users with the ability to reset the check engine light and monitor the effectiveness of any repairs or maintenance performed on their vehicle. This feature empowers users to diagnose and address potential issues promptly, ensuring the optimal performance and health of their vehicle.
